Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions our customers have.

search
How do I track my order? expand_more
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also find the tracking information in the 'My Orders' section of your account page. Simply click the link to see the live status of your delivery.
What is the estimated delivery time? expand_more
Delivery times vary based on your location and the delivery slot you choose. Standard delivery is typically within 2-3 hours of your selected time slot. You can see the estimated delivery window at checkout before confirming your order.
How do I change my delivery address? expand_more
You can change your delivery address before your order is out for delivery. Go to 'My Orders', select the active order, and click 'Edit Address'. Please note that address changes are not possible once the order has been dispatched.
What if an item is missing from my order? expand_more
We're sorry for the inconvenience! If an item is missing, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery through the 'Help' section in your account. We will arrange for a refund or a re-delivery of the missing item.
