Returns & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Here's everything you need to know about our returns and refund process.

search
Eligibility for Returns expand_more

Find out which items can be returned, which cannot, and the required conditions for a successful return.

  • Eligible items: Canned goods, dry pasta, snacks, cleaning supplies (unopened).
  • Non-eligible items: Fresh produce, dairy, meat, bakery items (final sale).
  • Condition: Items must be in the same condition you received them.
Return Timelines expand_more
  • You have 7 days from delivery to initiate a return.
  • Processing takes 3-5 business days after receipt.
  • Refunds appear in 5-10 business days.
How to Start a Return expand_more
  1. Go to 'My Account' > 'Order History'.
  2. Select the order and click 'View Details'.
  3. Choose item(s) and reason.
  4. Click 'Request Return'.
