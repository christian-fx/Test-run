Delivery FAQs

What if an item is out of stock? expand_more

You can specify substitution preferences at checkout. Choose "Best match," "Specific item," or "No substitutions." We'll only charge for what you receive.

Can I modify my order? expand_more

Yes, until shopping begins. Go to "Order History" > "Modify Order". You'll get a notification when we start picking your items.
